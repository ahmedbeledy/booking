Payments
How can your guests pay for their stay?
How Payments by LaCasa works
  1. Your guest pays through LaCasa with more options like PayPal, WeChat Pay and AliPay.
  2. We facilitate your guest's payment. You don't have to deal with fraud, chargebacks or invalid cards.
  3. LaCasa sends payouts to you. You'll receive a bank transfer by the 15th of each month that covers all bookings with a check- out in the previous month.
The payment charge

You cover the 2.4% payment charge. Of course, no fees are collected when you waive a fee to offer guests free cancellation.
